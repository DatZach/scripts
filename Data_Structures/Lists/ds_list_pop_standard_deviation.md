dl_list_pop_standard_deviation
==============================

$$\sigma{x}=\sqrt{\frac{\Sigma{x^2}-n\bar{x}^2}{n}}
\qquad \small \Sigma{x^2}=x_1^2+x_2^2+\dots+x_n^2
\qquad \bar{x}=\frac{\Sigma{x}}{n}
\qquad \Sigma{x}=x_1+x_2+\dots+x_n$$
